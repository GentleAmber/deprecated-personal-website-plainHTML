Games

I enjoy good games. Curiousity of games is a major inspiration for me to learn programming. My personal top 3 are:

- Disco Elysium
- Outer Wilds
- Divinity: Original Sin 2

Basically, I love games either with good narratives or with good gameplay where I can learn, explore or mess around. DE is the representative of good narrative, DOS2 has good gameplay, and OW has a perfect mix of the two aspects.

Exercises

Studies

I felt I needed to understand more about how computer works on my journey of learning programming. So I did a bit of Nand2Tetris course and played Turing Complete to help myself understand. I have learnt how single electronic components form into complex ones, what machine codes are, and how to programme with assembly language to achieve minor functions. It was a wonderful journey and I plan to keep learning about computers and programming in the future.

I like learning English as well. Because my mother is a dedicated English teacher, I started learning English at an early age, and it became a familiar language to me. Having said that, there is still a great number of vocabularies and phrases I have not known yet, and I enjoy discovering them.
